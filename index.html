Storm Clinch District 6 Championship with Dominant 9-0 Victory

The Tampa BAYshore Storm put the finishing touches on a remarkable District 6 Championship run with a commanding 9-0 victory over PCLL All-Stars at Carol Kennedy Field. Powered by timely hitting, dominant pitching, and outstanding defense, the Storm secured the title and earned their place as 2026 District 6 Champions.

"Championship teams respond when challenged. The Storm finished their title run with back-to-back shutout victories and brought the District 6 banner home to Tampa BAYshore."

Reese Merrill sparked the offense with a perfect 3-for-3 day at the plate, collecting three singles and driving in two runs. The Storm struck early in the second inning when Anna Stefany delivered a two-run single, helping Tampa BAYshore build momentum and seize control of the game.

The lead continued to grow in the third inning as RBI singles from Taylor Large, Catalina Currea, and Reese Merrill extended the advantage. Tampa BAYshore finished with nine hits and eight walks, consistently putting pressure on the PCLL defense throughout the afternoon.

In the circle, Anna Stefany was dominant. The right-hander struck out 12 batters while allowing just two hits and no runs across six innings. Arabella Ciampa, Anna Stefany, and Reese Merrill each drove in two runs as the Storm completed a championship performance from start to finish.

With the win, Tampa BAYshore capped off an unforgettable tournament run and now turns its attention to the next stage of All-Stars competition as District 6 Champions.
